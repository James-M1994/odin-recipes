French Fries

Description

A wonderful staple of greasy, crispy soul food
This recipe is incredibly quick and easy for those who want something unhealthy in a hurry

Ingredients

  • 4 Potatoes
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Peel each potato
  2. Slice potatoes into thin strips (or thick strips if that is your preference)
  3. Submerge potato strips into hot oil for 5 - 8 minutes or until golden brown
  4. Remove from oil and lightly apply some salt and it's ready!
