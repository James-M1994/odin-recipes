Fried chicken

Description

This classic downhome southern dish is crispy, greasy and pure bliss to enjoy at any time
and on this page we're going to show you how to make it in a few easy steps.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of beef
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pasta sauce

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles to desired consistency
  2. Place layer of noodles in pan
  3. Add layer of sauce on top
  4. Add layer of cheese
  5. repeat step 1 through 4 until lasagna pan is filled to the top
  6. Heat in oven at 400 degrees for 60 minutes
