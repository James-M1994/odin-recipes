Fried chicken

Description

This classic downhome southern dish is crispy, greasy and pure bliss to enjoy at any time
and on this page we're going to show you how to make it in a few easy steps.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken drumsticks
  • Flour
  • Cooking oil
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Crack two eggs in a bowl and whisk
  2. Place raw chicken drumsticks in egg wash
  3. Coat chicken in flour
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until coated with desired amount
  5. Place drumsticks in hot oil
  6. Let fry for about 15 minutes or until internal temperature is 160 degree farenheit
